About Me

I play bass. Basically all the time. Show tunes, rockabilly slap, fretless 80s pop, Stax-style R&B (the funkier the better), four strings, 12 strings, with a bow. I do it all, and you should hire me to come play bass for you, because if my wife has to listen to one more bass solo, I may be sleeping in the garage.
